Our Roots

Growing up in Louisville, my connection to the land started early. I spent summers working in local gardens, learning the rhythms of the seasons and the specific needs of our regional soil.

Our Roots

That early passion led me to the University of Kentucky, where I earned my degree in Landscape Architecture. I wanted to pair hands-on knowledge with formal design principles to create spaces that are both beautiful and structurally sound.

Our Roots

In 1999, I founded Scott's Landscaping with a simple premise: provide artisanal quality to every project, large or small. It's a commitment we still honor today.
